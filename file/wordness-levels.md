# ðŸ—£ "Wordness" levels

### Standard&#x20;

* words that all speakers of a "language" can understand

### Semi-standard

* common slang words or "normalized jargon" that do not appear in a dictionary

### Slang / Jargon

* words used by people only within a professional field or sub-culture

### Group jargon

* words used by people within only a group of acquaintances or friends
  * i.e. usually based on a "you had to be there" common experience&#x20;

### Two-people jargon

* words used in a context by only two people
  * i.e. no one else would know what these two people are saying!

### Intra-personal jargon

* words used by only the person who created them
  * e.g. a personal slang or even language used to write secret notes for later reference
