### Pre-flight

* enter airport terminal
* check-in with airline
  * at a desk with a service representative or an automated kiosk
    * show identification
    * weigh checked baggage
    * receive boarding pass
* clear security checks
* clear border patrol (if necessary)
* enjoy gate waiting area (or lounge!)
* shop at duty-free (if desired)

### Boarding

* board correct flight
* find assigned seat
* put away carry-on bags
* wear seat belt
* review safety procedures

### Flight

* prepare for take-off
* take-off
* _optional:_ order in-flight refreshments
* _optional:_ peruse in-flight entertainment
* _optional:_ peruse own entertainment
* _optional:_ take power nap(s)
* re-fasten seat belt (if necessary)
* prepare for landing

### Post-flight (non-connecting)

* disembark direct flight
* pass through customs (if necessary)
* claim checked baggage
* exit airport terminal

### Post-flight (connecting)

* disembark previous flight
* pass through customs (if necessary)
* pass through security
* enjoy gate waiting area (or lounge!)
* board connecting flight
* repeat "Boarding" steps
